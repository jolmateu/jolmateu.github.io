What can you do for the environment?

We have the effects of climate change in sight, hotter summers, colder winters, and many areas with uncontrolled floods, this is the problem but how can you help in the solution? You can do something?
The answer is yes, every day you use elements that can increase environmental pollution and this contributes to climate change, elements such as aerosols, fossil fuels, the indiscriminate use of energy, so what can you do in your home, use electrical energy in the proper way, if you can use clean energy such as solar or wind energy it will be a great saving, additionally you can stop using polluting aerosols and of course if using vehicles that are more environmentally friendly, if you can use your bicycle it would be fabulous, you help the environment and keep fit.
As well as these ideas there are many more, can you share one of your ideas to reduce pollution and help the environment? We await your answers?
