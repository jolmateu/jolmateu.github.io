Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the good use of natural resources, train, teach and transmit how environmental pollution is one of the factors that are influencing climate change, hotter summers, colder winters and often unusual weather, additionally we inform the weather for our region, as well as activities that can be developed outdoors to take advantage of each of the weather stations in our city. We provide tools for teaching environmental principles and sustainable communities focused according to the type of audience, educational workshops for children and young people, statistical talks for university students or activities for older people.
