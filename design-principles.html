WDD 230 - Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

On this page the highest hierarchy is the photo that is the most important to highlight the subject, then the meaning of the photo "Find your family. Discover yourself" which is the message you want to convey the page and then there is the button to create your account is a clear example of how hierarchy works to give focus.

White Space

The white spaces on this page allow you to see more organized and clean the presentation of the products, and allow you to focus on the photos and the action buttons that stand out, such as the one on WhatsApp.

Fitt's Law

On the xiaomi page, the orange buttons stand out, inviting us to action, they are very clean, they do not have other buttons around that interrupt the action, a clear example of Fitt's law.
