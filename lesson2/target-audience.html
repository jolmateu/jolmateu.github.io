Target Audience

The target audience is the population that lives in this city, all those who are interested in seeing the current climate or weather forecasts and additionally who wish to make changes in their habits to help take care of the planet in the climate field. It is appropriate for all ages, we have a section for children in which they will find activities for them to learn about climate change, weather stations, etc.

Persona

Andres Palacio age 30, He is a married industrial engineer who likes to ride a bicycle, he checks the weather to see if he can be transported in his favorite means of transport, and he also checks the news of the guided bicycle tours that take place on weekends in the outskirts from the city to attend with his family.

Laura Castelini age 10, She is a college student, she likes to consult the section for children since videos are published to learn how to recycle or make crafts with recyclable material, in addition to the weekend workshops to learn about the climate, and how we can help mitigate the climate change.

Scenarios

  • I want to ride my bike to work, will I have to wear a raincoat? Yes, the page provides the current weather and a forecast for the day.
  • I am an elementary school teacher looking for ideas to teach about climate change. Yes, Yes, we have a section for children with free-use educational material to teach about this topic.
  • I am a high school student looking for statistical data about the weather in the city. Yes, Yes, we have weather statistics for the city for the last three years.
  • I am an outdoor event planner, I need the weather forecast for the weekend. Yes, Yes, we have a weather forecast for the whole week.
  • I am a carrier I want to know if there are roads closed due to the weather. Yes, You will find in real time the roads affected by the weather.
