Site Name

Change the Weather

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information about the climate in our city, in addition to providing information about pollution, statistics on how climate cycles have changed over time in the city. Another objective is to promote the use of environmentally responsible transport, taking into account climate change, usage statistics and how the pollution index can change.
